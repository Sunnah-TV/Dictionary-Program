Help.
Press 1 followed by enter to add a word to the list.
Press 2 followed by enter to remove a word from the list.
Press 3 followed by enter to change a word or its translation.
Press 4 followed by enter to print all words in the list.
Press 5 followed by enter to print for help.
Press 6 followed by enter to exit the program.

When there is no dictionary file this file will be created.
You can add words faster by opening the dictionary.txt file.
!!!Between a word an it translation must be a ' '!!!
!!!After the translation there must be a ' '!!!
